Gold Health Insurance - Child Plan

Introducing The Wellness First Life Gold Health Series – Child Plan, a global health insurance plan designed specifically to cover your child’s medical needs. This plan is offered by Softlogic Life in partnership with AXA, a Fortune 500 listed company trusted by over 100 million customers worldwide.


Medical emergencies can arise unexpectedly, especially when it comes to our little ones. It’s crucial for parents to be prepared in advance, especially in Sri Lanka where accessing certain treatments can be challenging. The Good Health Series – Child Plan provides comprehensive coverage, ensuring that your child receives the best medical care, no matter where they are.


Don’t wait for an emergency to strike – be proactive and protect your child’s health with The Gold Health Series- Child Plan. Trust us, it’s a decision you won’t regret!



Benefits

Policy period

Policy period available from 5 - 40 years.

Variety of policy covers

Policy covers starting from LKR 100,000 for Gold Health Insurance - Child Plan.

Flexibility

Highly flexible range of premium payment options (monthly, quarterly, bi-annually or annually).


Hospitalization benefits

Hospitalization benefits inclusive of room charges, medical practitioner fees, theatre charges, ambulance charges, maternity benefits, etc. for both within and outside Sri Lanka.

No Claim Bonus

No Claim Bonus from 25% up to 100% of the sum assured for every claim-free year.

Additional covers

Expandable with additional covers apart from life insurance options for enhanced protection such as Retirement Plans, Children’s Investment Plans and more..
